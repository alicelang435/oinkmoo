Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is boba?

A: Boba, also known as bubble tea, is a Taiwanese drink that typically contains tea, milk, and chewy tapioca pearls. It's usually served cold and is a refreshing treat on a hot day.

Q: Do you offer vegan or dairy-free options?

A: Yes, we offer a variety of dairy-free and vegan options, including almond milk and fruit-based drinks. Just let us know your dietary restrictions and we can help you find the perfect drink.

Q: What flavors do you offer?

A: We offer a wide range of flavors, including classic black and green tea, fruit flavors like strawberry and mango, and specialty flavors like matcha and taro. You can also customize your drink with different sweetness levels and toppings.

Q: Do you have any promotions or discounts?

A: Yes, we offer promotions and discounts periodically. Follow us on social media or sign up for our newsletter to stay updated on our latest offers and promotions.

Q: Can I place an order for pickup or delivery?

A: Yes, we offer pickup and delivery options. You can place an order online or through our mobile app for convenient pickup or have it delivered to your doorstep.

Q: Do you cater for events or parties?

A: Yes, we offer catering services for events and parties. Contact us for more information on our catering menu and options.
